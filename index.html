Mention

This product is developed to aid students in the transitioning from virtual school, during the COVID-19 pandemic, to in-person schooling. Mention is designed to increase the organizational skills of students.

Features

Mention provides a way to send announcements to students across an entire club, class, or school. There is a hierarchy present, informing the entire chain of students, teachers, and others within the organization.

Students can receive notifications from their organization's coaches, teachers, or adminstrators. These notifications include announcements regarding important information, easing the transition from online to the in-person schooling.

Pricing

This application is free and available to the public, specifically developed for all students. No donations required.

Get Started

To get started, please click on the Sign Up button. This will send users to the Sign Up page, where they must register and then login. Once logged in, users will be able to access Mention.
